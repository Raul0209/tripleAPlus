Private Interest Foundation /
Trust Formation & Administration



If you are seeking efficient succession planning and wealth management structures we can assist in the incorporation of a Private Interest Foundation / Trust in various jurisdictions. These types of vehicles are part of a multi-entity structure which provide the outmost protection for an enduring legacy while keeping the founders and settlors wishes for coming generations.

We can assess on what type of structure is best for each family or individual, taking into account country of citizenship, country of residence, family and operational needs, assets to be protected and jurisdictions where these are located, among others.
